## What is Khoj?

**Khoj is a powerful, open-source AI app that acts as your personal knowledge assistant, helping you find information, generate ideas, and boost your productivity.** Whether you're a student, researcher, or professional, Khoj helps you organize, access, and interact with your knowledge more effectively. Check out the [original repo](https://github.com/khoj-ai/khoj) for the source code and more.

## Key Features

*   **AI Chat:** Converse with any local or online Large Language Model (LLM) such as Llama3, Qwen, Gemma, Mistral, GPT, Claude, and Gemini.
*   **Knowledge Retrieval:** Get answers from the web and your documents, including PDFs, Markdown, and more.
*   **Cross-Platform Access:** Access Khoj via Browser, Obsidian, Emacs, Desktop, Phone, or Whatsapp.
*   **Custom Agents:** Create AI agents with custom knowledge, personas, models, and tools.
*   **Automated Research:** Automate research tasks and receive personalized updates.
*   **Semantic Search:** Quickly and easily find relevant information with advanced semantic search capabilities.
*   **Multimedia Support:** Generate images, listen to messages, and more.
*   **Open Source & Self-Hostable:**  Run Khoj privately on your computer, or use our cloud app.

## What's New

*   **`/research` Mode:**  Start any message with `/research` to explore Khoj's experimental research mode.
*   **Custom Agents:** Create custom agents with tunable personality, tools and knowledge bases. [Learn more](https://blog.khoj.dev/posts/create-agents-on-khoj/).
*   **Performance Improvements:** Read about Khoj's excellent performance on retrieval and reasoning benchmarks. [Read more](https://blog.khoj.dev/posts/evaluate-khoj-quality/)
