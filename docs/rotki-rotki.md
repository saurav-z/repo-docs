## 📖 **Overview**

[rotki](https://rotki.com/) is a cutting-edge, open-source, and self-hosted portfolio management tool designed for the privacy-conscious crypto enthusiast. Unlike closed-source platforms, rotki empowers you to own your financial data by keeping it encrypted and stored locally. This ensures you maintain complete control over your assets and financial privacy, making it the ideal solution for managing your digital assets securely.  Explore the [original repository](https://github.com/rotki/rotki).

<div id="why-choose-rotki"></div>

## 🔑 **Why Choose rotki?**

*   **Privacy-Focused:** Your data is encrypted and stored locally.
*   **Open-Source & Transparent:**  Benefit from community-driven development and code transparency.
*   **Self-Hosted:**  Full control over your data and infrastructure.
*   **Multi-Platform Support:**  Available on Windows, macOS, and Linux.
*   **Powerful Analytics & Reporting:** Gain valuable insights with advanced reporting.

---

<div id="features"></div>

## ✨ **Features**

*   ✅ **Portfolio Overview:** Track balances across multiple platforms, blockchains, and exchanges.
*   ✅ **Graphical Insights:** Visualize historical data for informed decision-making.
*   ✅ **Transaction Decoding:** Understand blockchain transactions, exchange events and history.
*   ✅ **Data Customization:** Personalize your UI, currency, language, and more.
*   ✅ **PnL Reports:** Generate detailed profit/loss reports with customizable accounting settings.
