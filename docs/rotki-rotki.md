## ğŸ“– **Overview**

[rotki](https://rotki.com/) is an open-source, self-hosted portfolio management tool that puts your privacy first, offering a secure and transparent way to track and analyze your crypto and traditional financial assets.  Unlike closed-source alternatives, rotki keeps your data encrypted and stored locally, giving you complete control over your financial information. Join the community and take control of your financial data.

**Key Benefits of Using rotki:**

*   **Privacy-focused:** Your data, your control.
*   **Self-hosted:** Run it on your own hardware.
*   **Open-source:** Transparent and auditable.
*   **Comprehensive Analysis:** Deep dive into your portfolio performance.

For **advanced features**, check out the [Pro Version](https://rotki.com/products/) or explore the [Feature Coverage Page](https://rotki.com/products/details).
