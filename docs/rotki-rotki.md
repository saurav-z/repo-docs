<div id="overview"></div>

## 📖 **Overview**
[rotki](https://rotki.com/) is an open-source, self-hosted portfolio manager, analytics, and accounting tool designed for the privacy-conscious crypto enthusiast. Unlike closed-source competitors, rotki allows you to maintain full control over your financial data by storing it locally and encrypting it, providing a secure and transparent way to manage your crypto assets.  It offers powerful features for tracking your portfolio, generating P&L reports, and analyzing your crypto holdings.

**Benefits of Choosing rotki:**

*   **Open Source & Transparent:**  Community-driven and auditable.
*   **Self-Hosted:** Keep your data under your control.
*   **Privacy-Focused:** Encryption and local storage.
*   **Multi-Platform Support:**  Runs on Windows, macOS, and Linux.
*   **Powerful Analytics & Reporting:** Gain deeper insights into your finances.

**Explore advanced features with the [Pro Version](https://rotki.com/products/) and discover more details on the [Feature Coverage Page](https://rotki.com/products/details).**
