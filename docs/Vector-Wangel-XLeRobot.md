# XLeRobot: Affordable Embodied AI for Everyone

**XLeRobot is a low-cost, open-source dual-arm mobile robot designed to bring embodied AI and robotics to the masses, starting from just $660 and assembled in under 4 hours!** ([See the original repo](https://github.com/Vector-Wangel/XLeRobot))

## Key Features

*   **Affordable:** Starting from just $660 USD, making embodied AI accessible.
*   **Fast Assembly:**  Build your own robot in under 4 hours!
*   **Open-Source:**  Leveraging existing open-source projects for a collaborative approach.
*   **Dual-Arm Mobile Robot:** Designed for general manipulation tasks.
*   **VR Control:**  XLeRobot 0.2.3 (XLeVR) offers VR Quest 3 whole-body control.
*   **Simulation Support:** Get started quickly with XLeRobot Simulation.
*   **Comprehensive Documentation:** Organized tutorials, demos, and resources available on the [XLeRobot Documentation website](https://xlerobot.readthedocs.io/en/latest/index.html).

## Getting Started

> [!NOTE]
> This guide is designed to be beginner-friendly. Familiarity with Python, Ubuntu, and Git is recommended.

1.  💵 **Buy Your Parts:** See the [Bill of Materials](https://xlerobot.readthedocs.io/en/latest/hardware/getting_started/material.html).
2.  🖨️ **3D Print:** Follow the [3D printing guide](https://xlerobot.readthedocs.io/en/latest/hardware/getting_started/3d.html).
3.  🔨 **Assemble:** See the [assembly instructions](https://xlerobot.readthedocs.io/en/latest/hardware/getting_started/assemble.html).
4.  💻 **Software Setup:** Get your robot moving with the [software guide](https://xlerobot.readthedocs.io/en/latest/software/index.html).
