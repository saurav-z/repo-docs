**XLeRobot is a revolutionary, low-cost, open-source dual-arm mobile robot, making embodied AI accessible to all.**

## Key Features & Benefits

*   **Affordable**: Starting from $660 USD (excluding 3D printing, tools, shipping and taxes).
*   **Easy Assembly**: Total assembly time estimated under 4 hours.
*   **Open Source**: Built upon open-source projects like LeRobot, SO-100, Lekiwi, and Bambot.
*   **Dual-Arm Mobile Robot**: Enables complex manipulation tasks.
*   **Real-World Control**: Control your XLeRobot with an Xbox controller.
*   **VR Integration**: Supports VR Quest 3 whole-body control.
*   **Simulation Support**: Includes official simulation environments for testing and development.
*   **Modular and Customizable**: Adaptable to various hardware and software configurations.
