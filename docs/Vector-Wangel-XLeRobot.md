XLeRobot is an open-source project empowering anyone to explore the exciting world of embodied AI with a cost-effective, dual-arm mobile robot platform.

**Key Features:**

*   **Affordable:** Starting at just $660, XLeRobot makes embodied AI accessible.
*   **Easy Assembly:** Build your robot in under 4 hours.
*   **Dual-Arm Design:** Enables complex manipulation tasks.
*   **Open Source:** Benefit from community contributions and a transparent codebase.
*   **Simulation & Real-World Control:** Experiment in simulation and control your robot with keyboard, controller or VR.
*   **Comprehensive Documentation:**  Detailed guides for hardware, software, and assembly.
*   **Community Support:** Engage with the XLeRobot community on Discord and other platforms.

## 🚀 Get Started 🚀

Follow these simple steps to build your own XLeRobot:

1.  💵 **Buy Your Parts:** Refer to the [Bill of Materials](https://xlerobot.readthedocs.io/en/latest/hardware/getting_started/material.html).
2.  🖨️ **3D Print:** Print the necessary parts using the provided [3D printing](https://xlerobot.readthedocs.io/en/latest/hardware/getting_started/3d.html) instructions.
3.  🔨 **Assemble:**  Put it all together with the [Assembly Guide](https://xlerobot.readthedocs.io/en/latest/hardware/getting_started/assemble.html).
4.  💻 **Software Setup:**  Get your robot moving with the [Software Tutorials](https://xlerobot.readthedocs.io/en/latest/software/index.html).
