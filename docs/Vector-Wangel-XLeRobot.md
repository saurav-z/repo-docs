# XLeRobot: Affordable & Accessible Embodied AI for Everyone

XLeRobot is a low-cost, dual-arm mobile robot designed to bring embodied AI to your home, offering advanced capabilities at a fraction of the price of other robotic solutions.  **Build your own XLeRobot for as little as $660, with assembly taking under 4 hours!**  Check out the original repo:  [https://github.com/Vector-Wangel/XLeRobot](https://github.com/Vector-Wangel/XLeRobot).

## Key Features:

*   **Affordable:**  Starting at just $660, making embodied AI accessible.
*   **Fast Assembly:**  Assemble your XLeRobot in under 4 hours.
*   **Dual-Arm Design:** Enables advanced manipulation and household tasks.
*   **Open-Source:** Built upon the giants: [LeRobot](https://github.com/huggingface/lerobot), [SO-100/SO-101](https://github.com/TheRobotStudio/SO-ARM100), [Lekiwi](https://github.com/SIGRobotics-UIUC/LeKiwi), [Bambot](https://github.com/timqian/bambot)
*   **VR Control:** XLeVR Quest 3 whole-body control system available.
*   **Simulation Support:** Official simulation environment available for testing and development.
