## Key Features of XLeRobot:

*   **Affordable**: Starting at just \$660, XLeRobot makes advanced robotics accessible to a wider audience.
*   **Open-Source**:  Built upon the giants: [LeRobot](https://github.com/huggingface/lerobot), [SO-100/SO-101](https://github.com/TheRobotStudio/SO-ARM100), [Lekiwi](https://github.com/SIGRobotics-UIUC/LeKiwi), [Bambot](https://github.com/timqian/bambot) , XLeRobot encourages community contributions and customization.
*   **Quick Assembly**:  Get your robot up and running in less than 4 hours of assembly time.
*   **Versatile Control**: Control XLeRobot in real life with a keyboard, Xbox controller, or Switch Joy-Cons.
*   **Simulation Support**:  Explore and test your ideas in a simulated environment with updated URDFs and control scripts.
*   **Dual-Arm Design**: Enables complex manipulation tasks.
*   **Expandable**: Compatible with various hardware options, including different camera types.

## What's New

*   **Real-world Control**: Control XLeRobot in real life using keyboard/Xbox controller/Switch Joy-Con with zero latency. (July 30, 2025)
*   **Enhanced Simulation**: Improved simulation environment, with updated URDFs, control scripts, and support for various controllers, new hardware, and cameras. (July 8, 2025)
*   **Comprehensive Documentation**:  A dedicated documentation website provides organized tutorials, demos, and resources. (July 1, 2025)
*   **XLeRobot 0.2.0 Release**: Hardware setup, the 1st version fully capable for autonomous household tasks. (June 13, 2025)
