## Key Features

*   **Affordable:** Starting from just $660, XLeRobot brings embodied AI within reach.
*   **Quick Assembly:** Build your robot in under 4 hours!
*   **Dual-Arm Mobile Platform:**  Equipped with two arms and a mobile base for household tasks.
*   **Versatile Control:** Control XLeRobot with keyboard, Xbox controller, or Switch Joy-Con.
*   **Simulation Support:** Develop and test your robot in a simulated environment.
*   **Open Source:** Built upon the shoulders of giants like LeRobot, SO-100/SO-101, Lekiwi, and Bambot, offering open access to code and resources.
*   **Comprehensive Documentation:**  [Detailed documentation](https://xlerobot.readthedocs.io/en/latest/index.html) is available to guide you.

## Getting Started

1.  **Buy Your Parts:** Consult the [Bill of Materials](https://xlerobot.readthedocs.io/en/latest/hardware/getting_started/material.html).
2.  **3D Print:** Get the necessary parts from [3D printing](https://xlerobot.readthedocs.io/en/latest/hardware/getting_started/3d.html) instructions.
3.  **Assemble:** Follow the [assembly guide](https://xlerobot.readthedocs.io/en/latest/hardware/getting_started/assemble.html).
4.  **Software:** Get your robot moving by following the [software setup](https://xlerobot.readthedocs.io/en/latest/software/index.html) instructions.
