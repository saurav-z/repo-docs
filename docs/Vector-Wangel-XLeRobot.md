**XLeRobot** is a low-cost, open-source, dual-arm mobile robot designed to make embodied AI research and experimentation accessible to everyone. Based on open-source projects like LeRobot, SO-100, Lekiwi and Bambot, XLeRobot provides a platform for learning, building, and exploring the future of robotics.

<img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f9c454ee-2c46-42b4-a5d7-88834a1c95ab" alt="XLeRobot Front View" width="100%"/>

### Key Features:

*   **Affordable:** Starts at approximately $660 USD (excluding 3D printing, tools, shipping, and taxes).
*   **Accessible:** Build and assemble in under 4 hours.
*   **Open Source:** Leverages and builds upon the work of other open-source robotics projects.
*   **Dual-Arm Design:** Allows for complex manipulation tasks.
*   **Mobile Platform:** Enables navigation and interaction in a variety of environments.
*   **Real-World Control:** Control XLeRobot in real life with keyboard/Xbox controller/Switch joycon.
*   **Simulation Support:** Includes simulation environment with updated urdfs, control scripts (support Quest3 VR, keyboard, Xbox controller, switch joycon), support for new hardware and cameras, RL environment. Get started in 15 min.
