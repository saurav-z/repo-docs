# XLeRobot: Build Your Own Affordable Embodied AI Robot 🤖

XLeRobot is a groundbreaking project aiming to bring embodied AI to everyone, offering a household dual-arm mobile robot at an unprecedentedly low cost.  Get started building your own robot for as little as $660!  ([See the original repo](https://github.com/Vector-Wangel/XLeRobot)).

## Key Features

*   **Affordable:**  Starting at just $660 (excluding 3D printing, tools, shipping, and taxes), XLeRobot makes advanced robotics accessible.
*   **Rapid Assembly:**  Build your robot in under 4 hours!
*   **Dual-Arm Mobile Robot:** Designed for household tasks and general manipulation.
*   **Open-Source and Customizable:** Built upon the shoulders of giants and with an active community.
*   **VR Control:** Control the robot in real life with an Xbox controller or via VR.
*   **Complete Documentation:** Comprehensive documentation including tutorials, demos, and resources.
