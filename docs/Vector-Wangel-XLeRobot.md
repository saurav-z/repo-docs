# XLeRobot: Affordable, Open-Source, Embodied AI for Everyone

**XLeRobot is an open-source, low-cost, dual-arm mobile robot, bringing cutting-edge embodied AI to your home at a price comparable to an iPhone!** Learn more and get involved on the [XLeRobot GitHub](https://github.com/Vector-Wangel/XLeRobot) page.

## Key Features

*   **Affordable:** Starting at just $660 USD, making advanced robotics accessible.
*   **Fast Assembly:** Build your own XLeRobot in less than 4 hours.
*   **Open-Source:** Based on [LeRobot](https://github.com/huggingface/lerobot), [SO-100/SO-101](https://github.com/TheRobotStudio/SO-ARM100), [Lekiwi](https://github.com/SIGRobotics-UIUC/LeKiwi), and [Bambot](https://github.com/timqian/bambot), offering full control and customization.
*   **VR Integration:**  XLeVR (XLeRobot 0.2.3) allows for whole-body control using VR Quest 3, with web-based control and minimal dependencies.
*   **Simulation Support:**  Simulate and test your robot with included URDF files and control scripts.
*   **Comprehensive Documentation:**  Get started easily with detailed tutorials, demos, and resources on the [XLeRobot Documentation](https://xlerobot.readthedocs.io/en/latest/index.html) website.

## 🚀 Get Started 🚀

> [!NOTE]
> Beginner-friendly tutorial! Prior Python, Ubuntu, and Github knowledge is helpful.

1.  💵 **Buy Parts:** Refer to the [Bill of Materials](https://xlerobot.readthedocs.io/en/latest/hardware/getting_started/material.html).
2.  🖨️ **3D Print:** Follow the [3D printing guide](https://xlerobot.readthedocs.io/en/latest/hardware/getting_started/3d.html).
3.  🔨 **Assemble:** Build your robot with the [assembly guide](https://xlerobot.readthedocs.io/en/latest/hardware/getting_started/assemble.html).
4.  💻 **Software:**  Get your robot moving with the [software instructions](https://xlerobot.readthedocs.io/en/latest/software/index.html).
