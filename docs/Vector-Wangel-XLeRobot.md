# XLeRobot: Affordable Embodied AI for Everyone

**Build your own household robot for as low as $660 and less than 4 hours of assembly time with XLeRobot!** This project provides a cost-effective and accessible platform for exploring embodied AI and robotics. Check out the original repo [here](https://github.com/Vector-Wangel/XLeRobot).

## Key Features

*   **Low Cost:** Starting at $660, XLeRobot is significantly more affordable than other embodied AI platforms.
*   **Fast Assembly:** Assemble your robot in less than 4 hours!
*   **Open Source:** Built upon the work of many open-source projects, fostering a community for collaboration and innovation.
*   **Comprehensive Documentation:** Detailed documentation and tutorials are available to guide you through the build process and software development.
*   **Real-World Control:** Control your XLeRobot in the real world using a keyboard, Xbox controller, or Switch Joy-Cons with zero latency.
*   **Simulation Environment:** A robust simulation environment is available for development and testing using updated URDFs and control scripts (supports Quest3 VR, keyboard, Xbox controller, and Switch Joy-Cons).
*   **Community Driven:** Join the Discord and other community channels for support and collaboration.

## Getting Started

> [!NOTE]
> If you are new to programming, spend some time familiarizing yourself with basic Python, Ubuntu, and Github.

1.  **Buy Parts:** Refer to the [Bill of Materials](https://xlerobot.readthedocs.io/en/latest/hardware/getting_started/material.html).
2.  **3D Print:** Follow the instructions for [3D Printing](https://xlerobot.readthedocs.io/en/latest/hardware/getting_started/3d.html).
3.  **Assemble:** Follow the [Assembly](https://xlerobot.readthedocs.io/en/latest/hardware/getting_started/assemble.html) guide.
4.  **Software:** [Get your robot moving!](https://xlerobot.readthedocs.io/en/latest/software/index.html)
