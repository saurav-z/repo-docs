## Key Features

*   **Affordable:** Starting at just $660, XLeRobot offers a cost-effective entry into embodied AI.
*   **Rapid Assembly:** Assemble your robot in under 4 hours!
*   **Open Source:** Built upon the shoulders of giants like [LeRobot](https://github.com/huggingface/lerobot) and others, fostering community collaboration and continuous improvement.
*   **Dual-Arm Mobile Robot:** Designed for general manipulation, enabling a wide range of tasks.
*   **Active Development:**  Regular updates and new features are constantly added.
*   **VR Control:**  Control XLeRobot with VR for immersive and intuitive control.
*   **Real-World Control:** Control XLeRobot in real life with an Xbox controller. No wifi needed and zero latency.
*   **Comprehensive Documentation:** Well-organized tutorials, demos, and resources available on the [documentation website](https://xlerobot.readthedocs.io/en/latest/index.html).
