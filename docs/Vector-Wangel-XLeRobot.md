# XLeRobot: Affordable Open-Source Household Robot for Everyone

**XLeRobot is a low-cost, open-source dual-arm mobile robot designed to bring the power of embodied AI into your home, costing less than an iPhone!**  Check out the original repo [here](https://github.com/Vector-Wangel/XLeRobot).

## Key Features

*   **Affordable:** Starting from approximately $660, making embodied AI accessible.
*   **Fast Assembly:**  Assemble your robot in under 4 hours!
*   **Open-Source:** Built upon and inspired by LeRobot, SO-100, Lekiwi, and Bambot, allowing for customization and community contributions.
*   **Dual-Arm Design:** Enables a wide range of household tasks and manipulation capabilities.
*   **VR Integration:**  Control your robot in real-time using VR with minimal dependencies and setup.
*   **Simulation Support:** Official simulations available to get you started quickly.
*   **Comprehensive Documentation:** Extensive documentation to guide you through building, programming, and using XLeRobot.
