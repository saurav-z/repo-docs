## InvenTree: Your Open-Source Inventory Management Solution

InvenTree is a powerful, open-source inventory management system designed to track parts, manage stock, and streamline your operations.  ([View on GitHub](https://github.com/inventree/InvenTree))

**Key Features:**

*   **Comprehensive Inventory Tracking:** Manage parts, stock levels, and locations with ease.
*   **Web-Based Interface:** Access and manage your inventory from any device with a web browser.
*   **REST API:** Integrate InvenTree with other applications and systems through its robust API.
*   **Plugin System:** Extend functionality with custom plugins and integrations.
*   **Mobile App:** Access inventory information on the go with the companion mobile app.
*   **Multi-Platform Support:**  Deploy using Docker, bare metal, or DigitalOcean.
*   **Multi-Language Support:** Translate the application to your native language via community-contributed translations.
