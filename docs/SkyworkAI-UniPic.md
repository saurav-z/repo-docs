# Skywork UniPic: Unleash the Power of Unified Multimodal AI

**Skywork UniPic** offers cutting-edge, unified multimodal models that redefine image understanding, generation, and editing, all in one powerful package. ([Original Repository](https://github.com/SkyworkAI/UniPic))

## Key Features:

*   **Text-to-Image Generation:** Create stunning, high-fidelity images from text prompts.
*   **Image Editing:** Perform seamless inpainting, outpainting, and precise object manipulation.
*   **Image Understanding:** Leverage robust perception capabilities for a wide range of visual tasks.
*   **Efficient Architecture:** Benefit from optimized models designed for both high accuracy and easy deployment.
*   **Unified Modeling:** Experience the power of a single model capable of handling both perception and synthesis tasks.
*   **Two Distinct Modeling Paradigms:** Choose between Unified Autoregressive Modeling (UniPic-1.0) and Efficient Architectures with Diffusion Post-Training (UniPic-2.0 series) to best fit your needs.

## UniPic Models:

*   **UniPic-1.0:** 1.5B parameters, Unified Autoregressive Modeling for joint visual understanding and generation.
*   **UniPic-2.0 Series:** Variants including *SD3.5M-Kontext* and *MetaQuery*, built on efficient architectures with Diffusion Post-Training for state-of-the-art performance.
