## What is Skywork UniPic?

Skywork UniPic is a cutting-edge repository featuring state-of-the-art unified multimodal models. We offer two distinct modeling paradigms to cater to your needs:

*   **UniPic-1.0:** A 1.5B parameter model utilizing **Unified Autoregressive Modeling**, seamlessly integrating visual understanding and generation into a single transformer.
*   **UniPic-2.0 Series:** Leveraging **Efficient Architectures** and **Diffusion Post-Training**, with variants like *SD3.5M-Kontext* and *MetaQuery*, for superior performance in text-to-image generation, image editing, and multimodal reasoning.

## Key Features

Skywork UniPic offers a comprehensive suite of features, including:

*   **Text-to-Image Generation:** Create stunning, high-fidelity images from simple text prompts.
*   **Image Editing:** Effortlessly perform inpainting, outpainting, and complex object manipulation.
*   **Image Understanding:** Benefit from robust perception capabilities for various visual tasks.
*   **Efficient Architecture:** Experience optimized performance for accuracy and ease of deployment.
