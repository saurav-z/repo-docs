# Skywork UniPic: Unleashing the Power of Unified Multimodal AI

**Skywork UniPic** revolutionizes image understanding, generation, and editing with its advanced, unified multimodal models. (See the original repo here: [https://github.com/SkyworkAI/UniPic](https://github.com/SkyworkAI/UniPic)).

## Key Features

*   **Text-to-Image Generation:** Generate stunning, high-fidelity images from text prompts.
*   **Image Editing:** Perform seamless inpainting, outpainting, and precise object manipulation.
*   **Image Understanding:** Benefit from robust perception capabilities for a wide range of visual tasks.
*   **Efficient Architectures:** Experience optimized performance for both accuracy and ease of deployment.
*   **Unified Multimodal Modeling:** Utilizing a single transformer for both perception and synthesis tasks.

## Models

Skywork UniPic offers two distinct modeling paradigms:

*   **UniPic-1.0:** This model utilizes **Unified Autoregressive Modeling** with 1.5B parameters for joint visual understanding and generation.
*   **UniPic-2.0 Series:** Leveraging **Efficient Architectures** and **Diffusion Post-Training**, this series delivers state-of-the-art performance in text-to-image generation, fine-grained image editing, and multimodal reasoning. Variants include *SD3.5M-Kontext* and *MetaQuery*.
