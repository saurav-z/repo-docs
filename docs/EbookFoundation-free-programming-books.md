## Key Features

*   **Extensive Collection:** Access a massive library of free programming books, courses, and tutorials.
*   **Multi-Language Support:** Learn in your preferred language, with resources available in numerous languages.
*   **Organized by Subject & Language:** Easily find resources tailored to your learning goals, whether you're a beginner or an experienced coder.
*   **Community Driven:** Benefit from a community-maintained list, ensuring up-to-date and relevant resources.
*   **Interactive Learning:** Explore interactive tutorials and coding playgrounds to enhance your practical skills.

## About

This repository, originally inspired by a StackOverflow post, has become one of the most popular resources on GitHub for programmers worldwide, and is now maintained by the [Free Ebook Foundation](https://ebookfoundation.org).
