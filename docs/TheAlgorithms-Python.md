## Introduction: Learn Algorithms with Python!

**The Algorithms - Python** is a comprehensive repository offering implementations of various algorithms in Python, perfect for learning and educational purposes. ([See the original repository](https://github.com/TheAlgorithms/Python)).

## Key Features:

*   **Extensive Algorithm Collection:**  Explore a wide range of algorithms, covering various computer science concepts.
*   **Educational Resource:** Ideal for students, developers, and anyone interested in understanding how algorithms work.
*   **Python Implementations:**  All algorithms are implemented in Python, making them easy to understand and use.
*   **Community Driven:** Benefit from a community of contributors and learners.
*   **Open Source:**  Freely available for use, modification, and contribution under an open-source license.
