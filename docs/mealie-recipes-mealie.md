## Mealie: Your Self-Hosted Recipe Manager and Meal Planner

**Mealie is a powerful, self-hosted recipe manager that simplifies meal planning and grocery shopping, making cooking at home a breeze.** ([Check out the original repo](https://github.com/mealie-recipes/mealie))

### Key Features

*   **Effortless Recipe Import:** Easily add recipes by importing them from a URL or manually inputting your own.
*   **Intuitive Meal Planning:** Plan your meals for the week with a user-friendly meal planner.
*   **Smart Shopping List:** Generate a shopping list, organized by supermarket sections, based on your meal plan.
*   **Custom Cookbooks:** Organize your recipes with custom cookbooks, making it easy to find your favorites.
*   **Docker Deployment:** Deploy Mealie quickly and easily using Docker.
*   **Multilingual Support:**  Available in 35+ languages for a global user experience.

### About The Project

Mealie provides a user-friendly interface for managing your recipes, planning meals, and creating shopping lists. It features a REST API backend and a reactive frontend built with Vue.  Whether you're importing from online sources or creating your own family recipes, Mealie has you covered.
