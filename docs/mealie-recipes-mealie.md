## Mealie: Your All-in-One Recipe Manager & Meal Planner

Mealie is a self-hosted recipe manager that simplifies cooking, offering a modern, user-friendly experience for organizing recipes, planning meals, and generating shopping lists.  [Explore Mealie on GitHub](https://github.com/mealie-recipes/mealie).

## Key Features

*   **Recipe Import:** Easily add recipes by importing from a URL or creating them manually.
*   **Meal Planning:** Plan your weekly meals with the intuitive meal planner.
*   **Shopping List:** Generate and organize your shopping lists based on your meal plan.
*   **Cookbooks:** Categorize and group recipes into custom cookbooks.
*   **Docker Deployment:** Simple and easy Docker deployment.
*   **Translations:**  Available in over 35 languages for a global user experience.
*   **API:** Provides an API for integration with third-party applications.
