## Key Features

*   **Recipe Import:** Easily add recipes by importing from a URL or manually entering details.
*   **Meal Planning:** Plan your meals with an intuitive meal planner.
*   **Shopping List:** Generate and organize shopping lists based on your meal plan.
*   **Cookbooks:** Organize your recipes into custom cookbooks.
*   **Docker Support:** Easy deployment with Docker.
*   **Multi-language Support:** Available in 35+ languages.

## About Mealie

Mealie is a self-hosted recipe manager, meal planner, and shopping list application with a user-friendly Vue.js frontend and a REST API backend.  It makes managing your recipes a breeze, with features like automated recipe importing from URLs, a meal planner for easy weekly planning, and shopping lists to ensure you have everything you need for your meals.  Mealie is designed for the whole family!
