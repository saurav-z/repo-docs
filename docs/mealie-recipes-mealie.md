## Mealie: Your Self-Hosted Recipe Manager and Meal Planner

Mealie is a powerful, self-hosted recipe manager and meal planner that simplifies cooking and meal planning for the whole family, available on [GitHub](https://github.com/mealie-recipes/mealie). Easily organize your recipes, plan your meals, and create shopping lists all in one place!

*   **Recipe Imports:** Import recipes directly from URLs, or add your family recipes manually.
*   **Meal Planning:**  Plan your weekly meals with an intuitive meal planner.
*   **Shopping List Generation:** Generate organized shopping lists based on your planned meals.
*   **Cookbook Organization:** Organize recipes into custom cookbooks for easy access.
*   **Docker Deployment:** Deploy Mealie quickly and easily with Docker.
*   **Multilingual Support:** Access Mealie in over 35 languages.
*   **REST API:** Integrate with third-party applications through the API.

[Explore the Docs](https://docs.mealie.io/) | [View Demo](https://demo.mealie.io/) | [Report a Bug](https://github.com/mealie-recipes/mealie/issues)

## About Mealie

Mealie is a user-friendly application designed to streamline the process of managing recipes, planning meals, and creating shopping lists. Built with a reactive frontend (Vue) and a REST API backend, Mealie offers a seamless and enjoyable user experience. Whether you're importing recipes from the web or manually entering your family favorites, Mealie has you covered.
