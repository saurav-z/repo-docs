## Key Features

*   **Effortless Recipe Import:** Import recipes directly from URLs or manually enter your family favorites with ease.
*   **Intuitive Meal Planner:** Plan your meals for the week ahead with a drag-and-drop meal planner.
*   **Smart Shopping Lists:** Generate organized shopping lists, categorized by supermarket sections.
*   **Custom Cookbooks:** Organize your recipes into custom cookbooks for easy access and sharing.
*   **Easy Docker Deployment:** Deploy Mealie quickly and easily with Docker.
*   **Multi-Language Support:**  Enjoy Mealie in over 35 languages.

## About Mealie

Mealie is a self-hosted solution designed to simplify your cooking experience.  It features a REST API backend and a modern, reactive frontend built with Vue.js, ensuring a smooth and enjoyable user experience.  Whether you're importing recipes from the web, adding family favorites, or planning your meals, Mealie has you covered.
