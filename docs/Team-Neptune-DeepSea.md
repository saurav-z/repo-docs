## DeepSea: Enhance Your Nintendo Switch Experience

DeepSea provides a comprehensive suite of features to enhance your Nintendo Switch gaming experience. This all-in-one CFW package offers a range of functionalities, from game management to system customization.

### Key Features:

*   **File Transfer:** Utilize a background FTP server for seamless file transfers.
*   **Game Installation:** Install NSP & XCI files directly from your hard drive, Wi-Fi, or PC.
*   **Performance Tuning:** Overclock or underclock your Switch for optimal performance.
*   **System Management:** Update both OFW (Official Firmware) and CFW directly through homebrew.
*   **Homebrew Discovery:** Browse and install new homebrew applications via the Appstore.
*   **Savegame Management:** Manage your save data with ease.
*   **Cheating & Customization:** Cheat in games (use responsibly!), emulate Amiibo, and customize your controller experience.
*   **Controller Compatibility:** Use a wide range of third-party controllers.
*   **LAN Play:** Enable LAN play for multiplayer gaming, similar to Hamachi.
*   **Tesla Overlay:** Control all DeepSea features with the Tesla overlay (L1+DpadDown+RightStick).

**Discover more features and in-depth guides on our [wiki](https://github.com/Team-Neptune/DeepSea/wiki).**

## Getting Started with DeepSea

1.  **Follow the guide:** Hack your Switch by following the guide provided at: [https://switch.homebrew.guide](https://switch.homebrew.guide).
2.  **Download:** Download the latest DeepSea release.
3.  **Install:** Place the contents of the release onto your SD card.
4.  **Launch:** Send the Hekate payload to your Switch in RCM mode and launch the CFW.
