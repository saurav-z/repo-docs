## DeepSea: Your All-in-One Nintendo Switch CFW Solution

DeepSea is a comprehensive custom firmware (CFW) package for your Nintendo Switch, offering a wide range of features and enhancements to elevate your gaming experience.  Unlock the full potential of your Switch with DeepSea!

[**Visit the DeepSea Repository on GitHub**](https://github.com/Team-Neptune/DeepSea)

## Key Features

*   **File Management:** Integrated background FTP server for easy file transfers.  Install NSP and XCI files directly from your PC, smartphone, or network drives.
*   **Performance Tuning:** Overclock and underclock your Switch's CPU and GPU for optimized performance and battery life.
*   **System Updates:**  Update your OFW (Official Firmware) and CFW directly through homebrew.
*   **Homebrew App Store:** Discover and install new homebrew applications directly from within DeepSea.
*   **Save Game Management:** Easily back up, restore, and manage your game saves.
*   **Cheating:**  Utilize cheat codes for single-player games (please respect online gaming etiquette).
*   **Amiibo Emulation:** Emulate Amiibo figures for in-game rewards and features.
*   **Controller Support:**  Use a wide variety of 3rd party controllers with your Switch.
*   **LAN Play:**  Create a local network for multiplayer gaming, similar to Hamachi.
*   **Tesla Overlay:** Control all of DeepSea's features with a convenient Tesla overlay (L1 + D-Pad Down + Right Stick).

Explore the [wiki](https://github.com/Team-Neptune/DeepSea/wiki) for an in-depth look at DeepSea's features!

## Getting Started

1.  **Follow this guide to hack your Switch:** [https://switch.homebrew.guide](https://switch.homebrew.guide)
2.  **Download the latest DeepSea release** and copy the contents to your SD card.
3.  **Launch the CFW:** Send the Hekate payload to your Switch in RCM mode.
