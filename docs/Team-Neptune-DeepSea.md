## Unlock the Full Potential of Your Nintendo Switch with DeepSea

DeepSea enhances your Nintendo Switch experience with a suite of powerful features, offering unparalleled customization and control.  

**Key Features:**

*   **File Management:** Integrated background FTP server for easy file transfers.
*   **Game Installation:** Install NSP and XCI game files from various sources (hard drive, Wi-Fi, PC, etc.).
*   **Performance Tuning:** Overclock and underclock your Switch for optimized performance or extended battery life.
*   **System Updates:**  Update both your OFW (Official Firmware) and CFW directly through homebrew.
*   **Homebrew Discovery:** Discover and install new homebrew applications through the built-in app store.
*   **Savegame Management:** Comprehensive tools for managing your game saves.
*   **Cheating:**  In-game cheating capabilities (use responsibly, please avoid online cheating).
*   **Amiibo Emulation:** Emulate Amiibo figures for in-game benefits.
*   **Controller Support:**  Use a wide range of third-party controllers.
*   **LAN Play:**  Play local multiplayer games online.
*   **Tesla Overlay:** Access and control all features via the Tesla overlay (L1 + D-pad Down + Right Stick).

**For detailed information and to explore the best features, please visit our [wiki](https://github.com/Team-Neptune/DeepSea/wiki).**

## Getting Started with DeepSea

Ready to experience the enhanced capabilities of DeepSea? Follow these simple steps:

1.  **Follow the guide:**  Refer to this comprehensive guide to hack your Switch: [https://switch.homebrew.guide](https://switch.homebrew.guide)
2.  **Download the latest release:** Obtain the latest DeepSea package.
3.  **Install to SD Card:** Place the downloaded files on your SD card.
4.  **Launch the CFW:** Send the Hekate payload to your Switch in RCM mode to launch the CFW.
