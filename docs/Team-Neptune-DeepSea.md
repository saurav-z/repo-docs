## DeepSea: Your Complete Nintendo Switch Custom Firmware Solution

DeepSea provides a comprehensive custom firmware (CFW) experience for your Nintendo Switch, packed with features to enhance your gaming experience.

**Key Features:**

*   **File Management:** Utilize a background FTP server for easy file transfers.
*   **Game Installation:** Install NSP & XCI files directly from your hard drive, PC, smartphone, or Wi-Fi.
*   **Performance Tuning:** Overclock or underclock your Switch for optimal performance.
*   **System Updates:** Update both your OFW and CFW directly through homebrew.
*   **Homebrew App Store:** Discover and install new homebrew applications.
*   **Savegame Management:** Manage your game saves with ease.
*   **Cheating (Use with Caution):** Access game cheating capabilities (use responsibly and avoid online play).
*   **Amiibo Emulation:** Emulate Amiibo functionality.
*   **Controller Support:** Use a wide variety of 3rd party controllers.
*   **LAN Play:** Enjoy LAN play functionality, similar to Hamachi for your Switch.
*   **Tesla Overlay:** Control all features with the Tesla overlay (L1+DpadDown+RightStick).

**Explore DeepSea in Detail:**

For a deep dive into DeepSea's capabilities and features, check out the [DeepSea Wiki](https://github.com/Team-Neptune/DeepSea/wiki).

## Getting Started with DeepSea

To install DeepSea, follow these simple steps:

1.  **Follow the Guide:**  Consult the comprehensive guide at [https://switch.homebrew.guide](https://switch.homebrew.guide) to properly hack your Nintendo Switch.
2.  **Download & Install:** Download the latest release from the [DeepSea GitHub Repository](https://github.com/Team-Neptune/DeepSea) and place it on your SD card.
3.  **Launch CFW:** Send the Hekate payload to your Switch in RCM mode and launch the CFW.
