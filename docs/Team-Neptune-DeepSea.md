## DeepSea: Unleash the Full Potential of Your Nintendo Switch

DeepSea offers a comprehensive suite of features to enhance your Nintendo Switch gaming experience. From advanced customization to convenient utilities, DeepSea provides everything you need to take control of your console.

**Explore the full potential of your Nintendo Switch with DeepSea!**

### Key Features:

*   **File Management:** Background FTP server for easy file transfers.
*   **Game Installation:** Install NSP & XCI files directly from your hard drive, Wi-Fi, or PC.
*   **Performance Tweaks:** Overclock and underclock your Switch for optimal performance.
*   **Firmware Management:** Update your OFW & CFW directly through homebrew.
*   **Homebrew App Store:** Discover and install new homebrew applications.
*   **Save Game Management:**  Manage your save games with ease.
*   **Cheats:**  Integrate cheats into your favorite games. (Please respect online play and terms of service.)
*   **Amiibo Emulation:** Emulate Amiibo functionality.
*   **Controller Support:** Use a wide variety of 3rd party controllers.
*   **LAN Play:** Enjoy local multiplayer experiences with LAN play functionality.
*   **Tesla Overlay:**  Control DeepSea's features using the intuitive Tesla overlay (L1 + D-pad Down + Right Stick).

[Explore DeepSea's features in detail on our wiki.](https://github.com/Team-Neptune/DeepSea/wiki)

### Getting Started

To get started with DeepSea, follow these simple steps:

1.  **Follow the hacking guide:** Consult the [Switch homebrew guide](https://switch.homebrew.guide) to properly hack your Nintendo Switch.
2.  **Download the latest release:** Download the latest DeepSea release from [the official GitHub repository](https://github.com/Team-Neptune/DeepSea).
3.  **Transfer to your SD Card:** Place the downloaded files onto your SD card.
4.  **Launch the CFW:** Send the Hekate payload to your Switch in RCM mode and launch the CFW.
