## SearXNG: A Privacy-Respecting, Hackable Metasearch Engine

SearXNG is a powerful and customizable metasearch engine that prioritizes your privacy and gives you control over your search experience. Find out more on the [original repository](https://github.com/searxng/searxng).

### Key Features

*   **Privacy-Focused:** SearXNG does not track your searches, ensuring your privacy.
*   **Open Source:** Hackable and customizable to fit your specific needs.
*   **Metasearch:** Aggregates results from various search engines, providing comprehensive search results.
*   **Multiple Instances:** Ready-to-use instances are available at [Searx.space](https://searx.space).
*   **Docker Support:** Easy deployment with Docker images, including ARM64 and ARM/v7 architectures.
*   **Extensive Documentation:** Comprehensive user, admin, and developer documentation is available on the homepage ([https://docs.searxng.org/](https://docs.searxng.org/)).
*   **Translation Support:** Contribute to SearXNG's internationalization efforts via [Weblate](https://translate.codeberg.org/projects/searxng/).
*   **GitHub Codespaces:** Develop and contribute directly from your browser using GitHub Codespaces.
