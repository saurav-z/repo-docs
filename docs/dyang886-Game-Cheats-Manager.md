## Key Features:

*   **Intuitive Trainer Browsing:** Easily search and locate trainers for your favorite single-player games.
*   **Effortless Trainer Downloads:** Download trainers directly from within the application.
*   **Simplified Trainer Launching:** Launch your downloaded trainers with a simple click.
*   **Centralized Management:** Manage all your trainers in one organized location.
*   **Customizable Settings:** Adjust themes, languages, and trainer sources to personalize your experience.
*   **Trainer Management Panel:** Access settings for trainer updates, search data, download servers, and more.

## Getting Started:

### 1. Browse & Launch Trainers:

*   Use the search bar in the left column to find installed trainers.
*   Double-click or click "Launch" to start a trainer, or click "Delete" to remove it.

### 2. Download Trainers:

*   Search for trainers using keywords in the right column and press "Enter".
*   Double-click a result to download it directly.
*   Customize the download path by clicking the "..." button.

### 3. Trainer Management:

*   Configure settings like automatic updates, search data, and download servers within the "Trainer Management" panel.

### 4. Options Menu:

*   **Settings:** Customize themes and languages.
*   **Import Trainers:** Import existing trainers. (Imported trainers will not auto-update)
*   **Open Trainer Download Path:** Open the folder where your trainers are saved.
*   **Add Paths to Whitelist:** Add the trainer download path to your Windows Defender whitelist or manually configure other antivirus software.
*   **About:** View application version and project-related links.

## Installation:

1.  **Download:** Get the latest Windows (64-bit) installer from the [latest release](https://github.com/dyang886/Game-Cheats-Manager/releases).
2.  **Run:** Execute the installer and follow the on-screen instructions.
3.  **Launch:** Open Game Cheats Manager from your applications folder or start menu.
