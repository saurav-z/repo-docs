## Key Features

*   **Cross-Platform Support:** Compatible with Windows, macOS, and Linux, supporting NVIDIA and non-NVIDIA GPUs, with options for CPU or cloud APIs.
*   **Offline Mode:** Run entirely offline using local models for privacy and security.
*   **Versatile Clients:**  Web and desktop clients with desktop pet mode for constant companion on your screen.
*   **Advanced Interaction:** Visual perception, voice interruption, touch feedback, Live2D expressions, pet mode, and chat log persistence.
*   **Extensive Model Support:** LLMs (Ollama, OpenAI, Gemini, Claude, etc.), ASR (sherpa-onnx, Whisper, etc.), and TTS (sherpa-onnx, Coqui-TTS, Bark, etc.).
*   **Highly Customizable:** Simple module configuration, character customization (Live2D models, prompts, voice cloning), and flexible agent implementation.

## Key Highlights

*   **Real-Time Voice Interaction:** Engage in natural conversations with your AI companion.
*   **Visual Perception:**  Your AI can "see" you and your screen through camera, screen recording and screenshots.
*   **Customizable Appearance:** Use a custom Live2D model to give your AI companion a unique look.
*   **Offline Functionality:**  Maintain your privacy with models that run entirely on your device.
*   **Open Source & Community Driven**: Get the best of open-source, powered by a collaborative community

## Quick Start

Get up and running with Open-LLM-VTuber quickly.  See the [Quick Start](https://open-llm-vtuber.github.io/docs/quick-start) documentation for detailed instructions.

## Update

Use `uv run update.py` to update if you installed any versions later than `v1.0.0`.

## Uninstall

Remove the project folder. Any extra tools or models downloaded via ModelScope or Hugging Face should also be checked and removed.
