## Key Features

*   **Cross-Platform Compatibility:** Works seamlessly on Windows, macOS, and Linux.
*   **Offline Mode:** Converse with your AI companion without an internet connection.
*   **Web and Desktop Clients:** Choose your preferred way to interact, with features like a desktop pet mode.
*   **Advanced Interaction:**
    *   Visual perception with camera and screen recording.
    *   Voice interruption and touch feedback.
    *   Live2D expressions and pet mode with transparent backgrounds.
    *   AI's inner thoughts display and proactive speaking.
    *   Persistent chat logs and TTS translation support.
*   **Extensive Model Support:**
    *   **LLMs:** Ollama, OpenAI (and OpenAI-compatible APIs), Gemini, Claude, Mistral, DeepSeek, Zhipu AI, GGUF, LM Studio, vLLM, etc.
    *   **ASR:** sherpa-onnx, FunASR, Faster-Whisper, Whisper.cpp, Whisper, Groq Whisper, Azure ASR, etc.
    *   **TTS:** sherpa-onnx, pyttsx3, MeloTTS, Coqui-TTS, GPTSoVITS, Bark, CosyVoice, Edge TTS, Fish Audio, Azure TTS, etc.
*   **Highly Customizable:**
    *   Simple module configuration.
    *   Character customization with Live2D models, prompts, and voice cloning.
    *   Flexible Agent implementation.
    *   Modular design for easy extensibility.
