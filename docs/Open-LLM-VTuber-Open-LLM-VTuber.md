## Key Features

*   **Cross-Platform Compatibility:** Runs smoothly on Windows, macOS, and Linux.
*   **Offline Mode:** Complete privacy and security with local model support.
*   **Web & Desktop Clients:** User-friendly interfaces with desktop pet mode.
*   **Advanced Interaction:** 
    *   Vision with camera, screen recording, and screenshots
    *   Voice interruption and touch feedback.
    *   Live2D expressions and pet mode.
    *   AI inner thoughts display.
    *   Proactive speaking and chat log persistence.
    *   TTS translation.
*   **Extensive Model Support:**  LLMs (Ollama, OpenAI, etc.), ASR (sherpa-onnx, Whisper, etc.), TTS (sherpa-onnx, Coqui-TTS, etc.).
*   **Highly Customizable:** 
    *   Simple module configuration.
    *   Character customization: Live2D models, prompts, voice cloning.
    *   Flexible Agent implementation.
    *   Extensible modular design.

## Why Choose Open-LLM-VTuber?

Open-LLM-VTuber offers a unique and engaging way to interact with AI.  Customize your virtual companion to be a friend, partner, or whatever you imagine, all while leveraging open-source technology and running locally for ultimate privacy.
