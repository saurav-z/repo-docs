## Key Features

*   **Cross-Platform Compatibility:** Works seamlessly on Windows, macOS, and Linux.
*   **Offline Mode:** Enjoy complete privacy and security with local model support; no internet connection is needed.
*   **Web and Desktop Clients:** Utilize either web or desktop client modes with a variety of interactive features.
*   **Advanced Interaction:** Engage with your AI through:
    *   Visual perception (camera, screen recording, screenshots)
    *   Voice interruption
    *   Touch Feedback
    *   Live2D expressions
    *   Pet mode (transparent background)
    *   Display AI's inner thoughts
    *   AI proactive speaking
    *   Persistent Chat Logs
    *   TTS translation support
*   **Extensive Model Support:** Compatible with a wide range of LLMs, ASR, and TTS engines.
*   **Highly Customizable:** Tailor your AI companion's appearance and persona.

## Why Choose Open-LLM-VTuber?

Open-LLM-VTuber is your gateway to a personalized AI experience. With offline capabilities, a focus on privacy, and extensive customization options, you can create a unique virtual companion that fits your needs, whether you want a virtual girlfriend, boyfriend, cute pet, or any other character. Enjoy it anywhere, anytime!
