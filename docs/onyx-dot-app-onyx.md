Onyx, formerly Danswer, is an open-source AI platform designed to connect to your company's documents, apps, and people, providing intelligent search and knowledge management capabilities. You can find the original source code on [GitHub](https://github.com/onyx-dot-app/onyx).

## Key Features

*   **AI-Powered Chat Interface:** Interact with your company's knowledge base through a feature-rich chat interface, compatible with your preferred LLM.
*   **Extensive Connector Support:** Seamlessly integrate with over 40 popular platforms, including Google Drive, Slack, Confluence, Salesforce, and more, to keep knowledge and access controls synchronized.
*   **Custom AI Agents:** Create and deploy custom AI agents with unique prompts, knowledge sources, and actions tailored to your specific needs.
*   **Flexible Deployment:** Deploy Onyx securely on your laptop, on-premise, or in the cloud, scaling to meet your organization's needs.
*   **Deep Research Capabilities:** Perform in-depth research across your team's collective knowledge.

## Deployment

**Get started in seconds with a free trial on [Onyx Cloud](https://cloud.onyx.app/signup).**

Onyx can be deployed locally (even on a laptop) or on a virtual machine using a single `docker compose` command. Consult our [documentation](https://docs.onyx.app/quickstart) for detailed instructions.  We also provide built-in support for high-availability and scalable deployments on Kubernetes. See our [deployment documentation](https://github.com/onyx-dot-app/onyx/tree/main/deployment) for more information.

## Additional Benefits

*   **Advanced AI Models:** Leverage custom deep learning models for indexing and inference, continuously improving through user feedback.
*   **Robust Security:** Benefit from flexible security features, including SSO (OIDC/SAML/OAuth2), RBAC, and credential encryption.
*   **Knowledge Curation:** Utilize knowledge curation features such as document sets, query history, and usage analytics.
*   **Scalability:** Deploy Onyx at scale, tested for tens of thousands of users and hundreds of millions of documents.

## Roadmap
