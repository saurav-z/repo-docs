<p><strong><a href="https://www.onyx.app/">Onyx</a></strong> (formerly Danswer) is the AI platform designed to connect to your company's documents, apps, and people, providing powerful Gen-AI-driven search and knowledge management capabilities. This open-source solution offers a rich chat interface and seamlessly integrates with your chosen Large Language Model (LLM).</p>

## Deployment

**Get started in seconds with a free trial on [Onyx Cloud](https://cloud.onyx.app/signup)**.

Onyx can also be run locally or deployed on a virtual machine with a single `docker compose` command. Check out our [docs](https://docs.onyx.app/quickstart) for detailed instructions.  We offer built-in support for high-availability and scalable deployment on Kubernetes.
