## Key Features of Onyx

*   **AI-Powered Chat Interface:** Engage in natural language conversations with your company's knowledge base.
*   **Connect to Your Tools:** Sync knowledge and access controls across 40+ connectors like Google Drive, Slack, Confluence, and Salesforce.
*   **Custom AI Agents:** Create AI agents with unique prompts, knowledge, and actions tailored to your specific needs.
*   **Deep Research and Insights:**  Find the information you need with powerful search capabilities.
*   **Flexible Deployment:** Deploy Onyx securely anywhere ‚Äì on your laptop, on-premise, or in the cloud.
*   **Custom Deep Learning Models:** Leverages deep learning for indexing and improved search results.
*   **Robust Security:**  Includes SSO (OIDC/SAML/OAuth2), RBAC, and credential encryption.
*   **Knowledge Curation:** Features like document-sets, query history, and usage analytics.
*   **Scalable Architecture:** Designed for deployments from small teams to large enterprises (tested up to tens of thousands of users and hundreds of millions of documents).
