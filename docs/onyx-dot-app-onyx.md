**[Onyx](https://www.onyx.app/)** (formerly Danswer) is an open-source AI platform that connects to your company's existing knowledge base, applications, and team members, providing a powerful and unified search experience powered by generative AI.  Leveraging a feature-rich chat interface, Onyx seamlessly integrates with your preferred LLMs, allowing you to extract valuable insights from your data.

## Key Features

*   **AI-Powered Chat:** Interact with your company's knowledge through a user-friendly chat interface, utilizing the LLM of your choice.
*   **Seamless Integrations:** Connect to over 40+ popular applications and data sources with pre-built connectors, including:
    *   Google Drive
    *   Slack
    *   Confluence
    *   Salesforce
    *   And more... ([See full list](https://docs.onyx.app/connectors))
*   **Custom AI Agents:** Create tailored AI agents with unique prompts, knowledge access, and actions to automate tasks and streamline workflows.
*   **Flexible Deployment:** Deploy Onyx securely on any infrastructure: laptop, on-premise, or cloud-based.
*   **Deep Knowledge Research:** Conduct in-depth research across your team's collective knowledge.

## Deployment

Get started in seconds!

*   **Onyx Cloud:** Try Onyx for free on [Onyx Cloud](https://cloud.onyx.app/signup).
*   **Self-Hosting:** Deploy locally (even on a laptop) or on a virtual machine with a simple `docker compose` command. See our [docs](https://docs.onyx.app/quickstart) for more information.
*   **Kubernetes:** Supports high-availability/scalable deployment on Kubernetes. References [here](https://github.com/onyx-dot-app/onyx/tree/main/deployment).

## Other Notable Benefits

*   Custom deep learning models for improved indexing and inference.
*   Flexible security features like SSO (OIDC/SAML/OAuth2), RBAC, and credential encryption.
*   Knowledge curation features: document-sets, query history, usage analytics, and more.
*   Scalable deployment options tested up to tens of thousands of users and hundreds of millions of documents.
