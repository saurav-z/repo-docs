## Key Features of Onyx

Onyx (formerly Danswer) is an AI-powered platform designed to revolutionize how your team accesses and utilizes information.  Here's what you can do:

*   **AI-Powered Chat:** Engage in natural language conversations to find answers using your preferred Large Language Model (LLM).
*   **Deep Research:** Get comprehensive insights from your team's combined knowledge base.
*   **Connector Integration:** Seamlessly integrate with 40+ apps and services like Google Drive, Slack, Confluence, and Salesforce.
*   **Custom AI Agents:** Create tailored AI agents with unique prompts, knowledge, and actions to automate tasks and streamline workflows.
*   **Flexible Deployment:** Deploy Onyx securely on your laptop, on-premise, or in the cloud.
*   **Secure and Scalable:** Leverage advanced security features (SSO, RBAC, encryption) and scalable deployment options for organizations of all sizes.

## Core Benefits

*   **Enhanced Knowledge Discovery:** Quickly find the information you need within your company's data.
*   **Improved Team Productivity:** Save time and effort with automated workflows and AI-powered search.
*   **Data Security and Control:** Maintain control over your data with robust security features and flexible deployment options.
*   **Customizable AI Agents:** Tailor AI agents to specific needs and processes.

## Getting Started

*   **Try Onyx Cloud:**  Get started in seconds with a free trial at [Onyx Cloud](https://cloud.onyx.app/signup).
*   **Self-Hosting:** Deploy Onyx locally or on a virtual machine with a simple `docker compose` command. Detailed instructions are available in our [documentation](https://docs.onyx.app/quickstart).
*   **Kubernetes Support:** Deploy Onyx in a high-availability, scalable environment using Kubernetes.  See the deployment [documentation](https://github.com/onyx-dot-app/onyx/tree/main/deployment).

## Other Noteworthy Benefits

*   **Custom Models:** Deep learning models for indexing and inference, optimized through user feedback.
*   **Security Features:** Support for SSO (OIDC/SAML/OAuth2), RBAC, and credential encryption.
*   **Knowledge Curation:** Document sets, query history, and usage analytics.
*   **Scalability:** Tested for deployments with tens of thousands of users and millions of documents.

## Roadmap

We are continuously improving Onyx.  Key areas of focus include:

*   New information retrieval methods (StructRAG, LightGraphRAG, etc.)
*   Personalized Search
*   Expert Finder: Identify and suggest experts within your team.
*   Code Search
*   SQL and Structured Query Language support.
