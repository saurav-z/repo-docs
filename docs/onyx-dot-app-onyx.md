**[Onyx](https://www.onyx.app/)** (formerly Danswer) is the open-source, AI-powered platform that connects to your company's data, apps, and people, enabling powerful enterprise search and intelligent workflows. Seamlessly integrate with over 40 connectors and leverage any LLM of your choice. Deploy Onyx securely, anywhere, and at any scale ‚Äì from your laptop to the cloud.

## Key Features

*   **Intelligent Search:** Deeply research your team's knowledge using advanced AI search capabilities.
*   **AI-Powered Chat:** Engage in secure AI conversations using your preferred LLM.
*   **Seamless Integration:** Easily connect to your existing apps and data sources with over 40+ connectors.
*   **Custom AI Agents:** Create custom AI agents with unique prompts, knowledge, and actions.
*   **Flexible Deployment:** Deploy Onyx on your laptop, on-premise, or in the cloud.
*   **Knowledge Curation:** Utilize features like document sets, query history, and usage analytics.

## Deployment

**Get started in seconds with Onyx Cloud: [https://cloud.onyx.app/signup](https://cloud.onyx.app/signup)**.

Onyx can also be run locally or deployed on a virtual machine using a single `docker compose` command.  Refer to our [docs](https://docs.onyx.app/quickstart) for detailed instructions.

We also offer built-in support for scalable deployment on Kubernetes. Check out our [deployment](https://github.com/onyx-dot-app/onyx/tree/main/deployment) directory for more information.
