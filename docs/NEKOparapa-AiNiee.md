## ‚ú® **About AiNiee: The Ultimate AI Translation Tool**

AiNiee is a powerful and versatile AI-powered translation tool designed to effortlessly translate complex text from games, books, subtitles, documents, and more.  It streamlines your translation workflow with its user-friendly interface and advanced features.

## üîë **Key Features**

*   **Comprehensive Format Support:**
    *   üéÆ **Game Translation:** Deeply supports various game text extraction tools like Mtool, Renpy, Translator++, ParaTranzr, VNText, and SExtractor.
    *   üìö **Diverse File Compatibility:** Handles I18Next data, Epub/TXT ebooks, Srt/Vtt/Lrc subtitles, Word/PDF/MD documents, and more.

*   **Intelligent & Efficient:**
    *   üöÄ **One-Click Operation:** Drag and drop files, and AiNiee automatically detects file types and languages, simplifying your process.
    *   ‚è±Ô∏è **Blazing Fast Translation:** Get translations done quickly, saving you valuable time.

*   **Optimized for Long Texts & High Quality:**
    *   üéØ **Advanced Techniques:** Utilizes lightweight translation formats, chain-of-thought translation, AI terminology tables, and contextual association for accurate and coherent long-text translations.
    *   üíé **Quality Enhancement:** Offers features like prompt adjustment (basic prompts, character introductions, background settings, translation styles), one-click AI refinement, one-click AI layout, and terminology extraction for superior translation quality.

*   ‚ú® **Related Project:**  [**ReaDreamAI**](https://github.com/NEKOparapa/ReaDreamAI) - An AI-powered reading enhancement tool that generates high-consistency AI illustrations and videos for light novels and fan works.
