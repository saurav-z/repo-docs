## AiNiee: Your All-in-One AI Translation Toolkit

**Tired of tedious translations? AiNiee is your go-to solution for lightning-fast, high-quality AI translation of games, ebooks, subtitles, and documents.**

*   **Versatile Format Support:**
    *   üéÆ **Game Translation:** Deep support for popular game text extraction tools including Mtool, Renpy, Translator++, ParaTranzr, VNText, and SExtractor.
    *   üìö **Comprehensive File Handling:** Effortlessly process I18Next data, Epub/TXT ebooks, Srt/Vtt/Lrc subtitles, Word/PDF/MD documents, and more.

*   **Smart & Efficient Features:**
    *   üöÄ **One-Click Translation:** Drag, drop, and let AiNiee automatically detect files and languages‚Äîno complex setup needed.
    *   ‚è±Ô∏è **Blazing-Fast Results:** Get translated content in the time it takes to enjoy a soda.

*   **Optimized for Long-Form Content:**
    *   üéØ **Advanced Techniques:** Utilizing lightweight translation formats, chain-of-thought translation, AI terminology, and contextual understanding to ensure coherence and accuracy for long texts.
    *   üíé **Quality Control:** Customize translations with prompt adjustments for base prompts, character introductions, background settings, and translation styles. Includes one-click AI refinement, formatting, and terminology extraction.
