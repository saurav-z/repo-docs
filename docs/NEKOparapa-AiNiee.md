## AiNiee: Your All-in-One AI Translation Tool for Games, Documents, and More!

Tired of manually translating lengthy texts?  **AiNiee** is a powerful AI-driven translation tool designed to effortlessly translate games, books, subtitles, and documents, saving you time and effort.  Explore the following key features:

**Key Features:**

*   ‚úÖ **Versatile Format Support:**
    *   üéÆ **Game Translation:**  Deeply compatible with leading game text extraction tools like Mtool, Renpy, Translator++, ParaTranzr, VNText, and SExtractor.
    *   üìö **Broad Compatibility:**  Handles I18Next data, Epub/TXT ebooks, Srt/Vtt/Lrc subtitles, Word/PDF/MD documents, and more.
*   üöÄ **Intelligent and Efficient:**
    *   üñ±Ô∏è **One-Click Operation:**  Simply drag and drop files, and AiNiee automatically detects file type and language, eliminating manual configuration.
    *   ‚è±Ô∏è **Blazing Fast Translation:** Get your translations in minutes, freeing up your time.
*   üéØ **Optimized for Long Texts & High Quality:**
    *   üí° **Advanced Techniques:** Utilizes lightweight translation formats, chain-of-thought translation, AI terminology tables, and contextual awareness for accurate and fluent translations.
    *   üíé **Quality Enhancement:** Supports prompt adjustments (basic prompts, character introductions, background settings, translation styles), one-click AI refinement, formatting, and terminology extraction for superior translation quality.
