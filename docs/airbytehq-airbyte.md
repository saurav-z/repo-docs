## Airbyte: Open-Source Data Integration for ELT Pipelines

Airbyte is an open-source data integration platform designed to simplify the process of building Extract, Load, Transform (ELT) pipelines. With a vast library of pre-built connectors and a commitment to open-source, Airbyte empowers data engineers to move data from any source to any destination.

**[Explore the Airbyte GitHub Repository](https://github.com/airbytehq/airbyte)**

### Key Features

*   **Extensive Connector Library:** Access a growing catalog of 300+ pre-built connectors for APIs, databases, data warehouses, and data lakes. ([Connector Registry](https://connectors.airbyte.com/files/generated_reports/connector_registry_report.html))
*   **Open Source and Customizable:** Benefit from a fully open-source solution, allowing you to customize connectors and adapt to your specific needs.
*   **No-Code & Low-Code Connector Development:** Quickly build connectors with our no-code Connector Builder or low-code CDK.
*   **Flexible Deployment Options:** Deploy Airbyte open-source or use [Airbyte Cloud](https://cloud.airbyte.com/signup).
*   **Integration with Popular Tools:** Orchestrate your data pipelines with Airflow, Prefect, Dagster, Kestra, or the Airbyte API.
*   **Active Community:** Engage with the Airbyte community on [Slack](https://airbyte.com/community) and the [Airbyte Forum](https://github.com/airbytehq/airbyte/discussions).

### Getting Started

*   **Deploy Airbyte:** [Deploy Airbyte Open Source](https://docs.airbyte.com/quickstart/deploy-airbyte) or set up [Airbyte Cloud](https://docs.airbyte.com/cloud/getting-started-with-airbyte-cloud) to start centralizing your data.
*   **Build Connectors:** Create connectors in minutes with our [no-code Connector Builder](https://docs.airbyte.com/connector-development/connector-builder-ui/overview) or [low-code CDK](https://docs.airbyte.com/connector-development/config-based/low-code-cdk-overview).
*   **Explore Tutorials:** Discover popular use cases in our [tutorials](https://airbyte.com/tutorials).
*   **Orchestrate Syncs:** Orchestrate Airbyte syncs with [Airflow](https://docs.airbyte.com/operator-guides/using-the-airflow-airbyte-operator), [Prefect](https://docs.airbyte.com/operator-guides/using-prefect-task), [Dagster](https://docs.airbyte.com/operator-guides/using-dagster-integration), [Kestra](https://docs.airbyte.com/operator-guides/using-kestra-plugin), or the [Airbyte API](https://reference.airbyte.com/reference/start).
*   **Demo App:** Try it out yourself with our [demo app](https://demo.airbyte.io/)
