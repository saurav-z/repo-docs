## Airbyte: Open-Source Data Integration for ELT Pipelines

Airbyte is a leading open-source data integration platform, offering a comprehensive solution for building Extract, Load, and Transform (ELT) pipelines.  Move data effortlessly from diverse sources to your preferred destinations with Airbyte's extensive connector catalog and flexible deployment options.  **[Get started with Airbyte on GitHub](https://github.com/airbytehq/airbyte).**

### Key Features

*   **Extensive Connector Library:** Access a vast and ever-growing library of 300+ pre-built connectors for APIs, databases, data warehouses, and data lakes.
*   **Open-Source & Customizable:** Benefit from an open-source solution that empowers you to customize existing connectors and contribute to the community.
*   **User-Friendly Interface:** Deploy Airbyte Open Source or use Airbyte Cloud and build data pipelines in minutes.
*   **Connector Builder:** Quickly create connectors with our no-code Connector Builder or low-code CDK.
*   **Orchestration:** Integrate with popular orchestration tools like Airflow, Prefect, Dagster, Kestra, and the Airbyte API.
*   **ELT Pipelines:** Airbyte supports full ELT pipelines from APIs, databases & files to databases, warehouses & lakes.

### Getting Started

*   **Deploy Airbyte:** Deploy Airbyte Open Source ([Quickstart Guide](https://docs.airbyte.com/quickstart/deploy-airbyte)) or set up [Airbyte Cloud](https://docs.airbyte.com/cloud/getting-started-with-airbyte-cloud) to start centralizing your data.
*   **Build Connectors:** Create connectors in minutes with our [no-code Connector Builder](https://docs.airbyte.com/connector-development/connector-builder-ui/overview) or [low-code CDK](https://docs.airbyte.com/connector-development/config-based/low-code-cdk-overview).
*   **Explore Use Cases:** Explore popular use cases in our [tutorials](https://airbyte.com/tutorials).
*   **Orchestrate with your tools:** Integrate with [Airflow](https://docs.airbyte.com/operator-guides/using-the-airflow-airbyte-operator), [Prefect](https://docs.airbyte.com/operator-guides/using-prefect-task), [Dagster](https://docs.airbyte.com/operator-guides/using-dagster-integration), [Kestra](https://docs.airbyte.com/operator-guides/using-kestra-plugin), or the [Airbyte API](https://reference.airbyte.com/reference/start).

Try it out yourself with our [demo app](https://demo.airbyte.io/), visit our [full documentation](https://docs.airbyte.com/), and learn more about [recent announcements](https://airbyte.com/blog-categories/company-updates). See our [registry](https://connectors.airbyte.com/files/generated_reports/connector_registry_report.html) for a full list of connectors already available in Airbyte or Airbyte Cloud.
