# Find Thousands of Free Public APIs for Your Next Project

This repository is a **treasure trove** of free public APIs, meticulously curated by community members like you, and supported by [APILayer](https://apilayer.com/?utm_source=Github&utm_medium=Referral&utm_campaign=Public-apis-repo). Explore a vast collection of APIs from diverse domains to power your projects, all available to use at no cost.  [View the original repository on GitHub](https://github.com/public-apis/public-apis).

**Key Features:**

*   **Extensive Library:** Access a huge selection of free APIs across various categories.
*   **Community Driven:** Maintained and updated by a community of developers, ensuring accuracy and relevance.
*   **Easy Integration:** APIs designed to be easily integrated into your existing projects.
*   **Diverse Categories:** Explore APIs in areas like weather, sports, finance, and more.
*   **No Cost:**  Use these APIs for free, enabling rapid prototyping and development.
