## HelloGitHub: Discover Awesome Open Source Projects

**HelloGitHub** is your monthly guide to exciting and beginner-friendly open-source projects on GitHub. Explore a curated selection of projects to ignite your passion for open source!

[View the original repository on GitHub](https://github.com/521xueweihan/HelloGitHub)

### Key Features:

*   **Curated List:** Discover interesting open-source projects, perfect for beginners.
*   **Monthly Updates:** Stay informed with a new issue published every month on the 28th.
*   **Diverse Content:** Explore a wide range of projects, including software, books, practical projects, and enterprise-level applications.
*   **Beginner-Friendly:** Find projects designed to get you started with open source quickly.
*   **Multiple Formats:** Access the content on the [official website](https://hellogithub.com/) or the [HelloGitHub WeChat Official Account](https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/521xueweihan/img_logo@main/logo/weixin.png).
