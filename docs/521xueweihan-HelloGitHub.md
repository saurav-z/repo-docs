# HelloGitHub: Discover Awesome Open-Source Projects (Updated Monthly!)

**HelloGitHub** is your gateway to the exciting world of open-source, providing a curated collection of interesting and beginner-friendly projects every month.

## Key Features:

*   **Monthly Updates:** Discover new open-source projects on the 28th of each month.
*   **Beginner-Friendly:** Focus on projects that are easy to understand and get started with.
*   **Diverse Content:** Explore a wide range of projects, including practical applications, open-source books, and enterprise-level solutions.
*   **Inspiring Learning:** Get a taste of the open-source world in a short time, and find joy in exploring open-source projects.
*   **Community:** Discuss via [Wechat group](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/521xueweihan/img_logo/master/logo/weixin.png)

## What You'll Find:

HelloGitHub curates a selection of fascinating open-source projects covering various categories:

*   Interesting Projects
*   Open-Source Books
*   Practical Projects
*   Enterprise-Level Projects
