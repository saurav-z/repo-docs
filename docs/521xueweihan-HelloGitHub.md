## What is HelloGitHub?

HelloGitHub is a curated monthly newsletter and repository that showcases **fun and accessible open-source projects** on GitHub, perfect for those new to the world of open-source development.  We focus on projects that are easy to get started with, allowing you to quickly experience the joy of open source.

## Key Features

*   **Curated Monthly Updates:**  Discover new and exciting projects every month, released on the 28th.
*   **Beginner-Friendly Projects:**  Focus on projects suitable for those new to open-source.
*   **Diverse Content:** Explore a variety of projects, including open-source books, practical projects, and enterprise-level applications.
*   **Find the Joy of Open Source:** Designed to help you quickly understand and love open-source development.
*   **Active Community:** Join the WeChat group (link above) to connect with other enthusiasts.

## What's Included

Each monthly issue of HelloGitHub features:

*   **Interesting Open Source Projects:** Carefully selected projects across various domains.
*   **Open Source Books:** Recommendations for valuable reading material.
*   **Practical Projects:** Real-world projects to learn from.
*   **Enterprise-Level Projects:** Insights into how open-source is used in the industry.
