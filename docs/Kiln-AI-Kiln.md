## Key Benefits

Kiln streamlines AI development with these key advantages:

*   **Rapid Prototyping:**  Quickly test and compare different AI approaches with easy-to-use tools.  Kiln supports prompt techniques, diverse models, fine-tuning, and evaluations.
*   **Seamless Collaboration:**  Bridge the gap between technical and non-technical team members by providing intuitive tools for subject matter experts to contribute.
*   **High-Quality AI Products:**  Create and iterate on datasets to improve model quality, adapt to changing needs, and fix bugs.
