## Learn More

### Rapid AI Prototyping

Kiln makes it easy to try a variety of AI approaches and compare them without writing code.

### Collaborate Across Teams

Kiln bridges the gap between technical and non-technical teams. Subject matter experts create structured datasets; data scientists dive in with our python library.

### Build High-Quality AI Products

Every time you use Kiln, you gather the inputs, outputs, human ratings, feedback, and repairs needed to build quality models for your product.
