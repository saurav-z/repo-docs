## What is Kiln?

**Kiln is a powerful, user-friendly platform designed to streamline your AI development workflow, offering features for rapid prototyping, dataset collaboration, and model fine-tuning, all in one place.** Learn more on the [original repo](https://github.com/Kiln-AI/Kiln).

## Rapid AI Prototyping

Accelerate your AI development with Kiln.

### Key Capabilities:

*   **Various prompting techniques:** Basic, few-shot, multi-shot, repair & feedback.
*   **Chain of thought:** Include "thinking" instructions.
*   **Model Support:** Integrate with GPT, Llama, Claude, Gemini, Mistral, Gemma, Phi, and more.
*   **Fine-Tuning:** Create custom models using your Kiln dataset.

### Future Enhancements:

Kiln is evolving with plans to add no-code options for evals and RAG. Data scientists can currently create these today using Kiln datasets and our python library.

## Collaboration Across Teams

Kiln bridges the gap between subject matter experts and technical teams.

*   **Subject Matter Experts:** Use intuitive desktop apps to generate structured datasets and ratings without coding.
*   **Data Scientists:** Access and work with datasets created by subject matter experts through the UI or Python library.
*   **QA and PM:** Identify issues and generate data to improve model performance.

Dataset file format is designed for Git-based collaboration. Parallel contributions are managed through UUIDs, with attribution captured inside the dataset files.

## Build High-Quality AI Products

Kiln facilitates the creation of high-quality datasets crucial for AI product success.

### Benefits:

*   Kiln captures the inputs, outputs, human ratings, feedback, and repairs needed to create high-quality models.
*   Model quality automatically improves as the dataset grows, by giving the models more examples of quality content and mistakes.
*   Iterate the dataset to address shifting product goals or new bugs.
