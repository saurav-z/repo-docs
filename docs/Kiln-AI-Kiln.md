## Learn More

### Rapid AI Prototyping

Kiln empowers you to swiftly experiment with various AI approaches and compare them without writing code, leading to enhanced model quality and performance.

Key features include support for:

-   Diverse prompting techniques: basic, few-shot, multi-shot, repair & feedback.
-   Chain of thought / thinking with custom instructions.
-   Wide model compatibility: GPT, Llama, Claude, Gemini, Mistral, Gemma, Phi.
-   Fine Tuning to create custom models with your Kiln dataset
-   LLM-as-Judge and G-Eval evaluation methods
-   Distilling models

RAG is coming soon!

### Seamless Collaboration

Kiln is a collaboration tool that bridges the gap between technical and non-technical teams.

Subject matter experts can easily create structured datasets and ratings using our intuitive desktop apps, without coding or command-line interfaces.

Data scientists can use our UI or our Python library to consume the datasets created by subject matter experts.

QA and PM can also identify issues.

Kiln's dataset file format, designed for Git, promotes efficient parallel contribution, and allows for non-technical team members to contribute data and evals, even without Git.

### Build High Quality AI Products with Datasets

Kiln enables the creation of AI-ready datasets, capturing essential inputs, outputs, human ratings, and feedback to build quality models for your product. Data-driven improvements are automated as the dataset grows. The dataset can be easily iterated and maintained to address new challenges.

Use our synthetic data generation tool to efficiently build datasets for evals and fine-tuning.
