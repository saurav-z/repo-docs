# Kiln: Accelerate AI Development with Rapid Prototyping and Collaborative Datasets

Kiln is a powerful toolkit designed to streamline AI development, enabling rapid prototyping, efficient dataset collaboration, and the creation of high-quality AI products.  [Visit the original repository](https://github.com/Kiln-AI/Kiln).

*   **üéõÔ∏è Fine-Tuning**:  Zero-code fine-tuning for models like Llama, GPT-4o, and others, with automatic serverless model deployment.
*   **üìä Model Evaluation (Evals)**: Assess your AI models with state-of-the-art evaluators, ensuring optimal performance and quality.

## Learn More

### Rapid Prototyping

Kiln simplifies experimentation and comparison of various AI approaches, allowing you to find the best solutions quickly without extensive coding. It supports:

*   Diverse prompting techniques
*   Chain-of-thought functionality
*   Integration with numerous models
*   Fine-tuning capabilities

### Collaborative AI Development

Kiln bridges the gap between technical and non-technical teams, enabling subject matter experts to generate datasets and ratings, while data scientists can leverage these datasets with the UI or the Python library.

### Build High-Quality AI Products with Datasets

Kiln helps you create a dataset from product usage. The more you use Kiln, the better your AI products will become.
